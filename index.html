Bio

As a grown up, I chose to be on the other side of the screen I was hooked to as a child.

I attended Rutgers Business School where I earned a Bachelor's Degree in Finance, excelled in software engineering coursework, and pursued internships that strengthened my programming, cyber-security and mathematical skills. I supported myself through college as head host of the busiest restaurant in New Jersey where I developed excellent interpersonal skills and learned how to present myself and my product in a way that resonates to a customer's core. In order to nourish STEM skills in my community, I delayed starting my career in the private sector to teach mathematics at my childhood middle school. I then chose to reinforce my software engineering skills at a full-time, intensive software engineering program called App Academy.

I'm looking to join a team that builds technology with a pulse that reverberates to the depths of the client.
